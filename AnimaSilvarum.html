Anima Silvarum

A 2D action-exploration adventure set in a colorful world full of mysteries and enigmatic secrets. Take control of Héctor, an apprentice scribe who is unexpectedly thrown into a criminal conspiracy involving robbery and a kidnapping. Investigate unexplored territories as you venture outside your village and into the uncharted realms beyond the forest. Defeat mysterious enemies, solve engaging puzzles, and recover what is yours by discovering the truth behind the crimes!

Release Date: 24 October 2023
Team Size: 4 programmers, 11 artists
Language: C++
Engine: 2D game engine done from scratch in C++

Download

My Role

I worked for this game at my second year at DigiPen, with the role of Gameplay programmer. The most relevant task I worked on where the following:
  • Implemented the collision and physics systems
  • Created the level editor using ImGui and ImGuizmo, implementing the basics of object creation and deletion, component management, scene creation and loading, as well as many tools to help the artists better create levels, like ctrl z, vertex snapping or atlas loading.
  • Implemented text rendering and translation tools, including managing fonts using non latin scrips.
  • For gameplay, I worked on the quest system, final boss, and UI work like the menus and game UI.
