Ant Squad

Ant Squad is a Top-down 2D reverse tower defence game where the player controls a nest of ants, and he has to send them throughout the map, so they reach to the end of the same. The player will have three different type of units and will be able to send them through three different paths. At the end of the map there is the final boss and alongside the three paths there are enemies to delay the advance of the player.
