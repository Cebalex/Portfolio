Ant Squad

Ant Squad is a Top-down 2D reverse tower defence game where the player controls a nest of ants, and he has to send them throughout the map, so they reach to the end of the same. The player will have three different type of units and will be able to send them through three different paths. At the end of the map there is the final boss and alongside the three paths there are enemies to delay the advance of the player.

Release Date: 30 april 2022
Team Size: 4 programmers
Language: C++
Engine: 2D engine provided by DigiPen

My Role

This was my second game I worked on my first year at DigiPen, with the role of Game Programmer, altough I also worked a lot on the design of the game. The most relevant task I worked on where the following:
  • Design of the game mechanics and balancing the game economy system.
  • Programmed the power ups of the player.
  • Made the UI of the player and enemy units, the power up and path selection buttons.
