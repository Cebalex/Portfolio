Ithangi

The game is a 2D top-down shooter where you control a tank that will fight against other enemy tanks. There are 3 different levels, and the objective is unique to each one of them, as well as the terrains, which have unique mechanics. You can also play with a different tank depending on the level. The thematic of the game is based in battles of the second world war and their tanks.
