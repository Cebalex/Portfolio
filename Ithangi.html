Ithangi

The game is a 2D top-down shooter where you control a tank that will fight against other enemy tanks. There are 3 different levels, and the objective is unique to each one of them, as well as the terrains, which have unique mechanics. You can also play with a different tank depending on the level. The thematic of the game is based in battles of the second world war and their tanks.

Release Date: 23 December 2021
Team Size: 3 programmers
Language: C#
Engine: Unity

My Role

This was my first game I worked on at DigiPen, with the role of Game Designer. The most relevant task I worked on where the following:
  • Design of the game mechanics, enemy types and level design.
  • Programmed the player movement and main mechanics like shooting and power ups.
  • Made the UI of the player and menus.
